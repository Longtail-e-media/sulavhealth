Ancient Greeks have evolved therapeutics with herbs since 3000 B.C. and the first written proofs are found in Orphica (poetry collection by Orpheus) and in Homeric poems, where the use of pharmaceutical herbs is described in detail (e.g. mandrake, dittany, crocus (saffron) etc). Asclepius, Hippocrates and Pythagoras had great knowledge of pharmaceutical herbs and were using them to heal people. Hippocrates was the first to systemize his medicinal knowledge in organized manuscripts, where he mentions the use of about 236 herbs.
